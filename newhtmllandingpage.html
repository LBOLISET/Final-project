Welcome to LAMORA Resorts

Nestled in the heart of Singapore’s bustling Orchard Road, our hotel provides an ideal base for immersing yourself in the city’s premium shopping and entertainment district. We are conveniently located near iconic destinations like Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and Universal Studios Singapore. Delight in our five innovative dining options as you soak up the vibrant pulse of the city.
